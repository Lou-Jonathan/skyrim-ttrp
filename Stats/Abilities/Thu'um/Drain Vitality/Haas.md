# Third Word : Haas (Health)
---
- **Uttering time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30-foot cone
- **Duration:** 3 minutes

The creature must make a Constitution saving throw or suffer the effect of the first two words, as well as take 2d6 points of necrotic damage.
