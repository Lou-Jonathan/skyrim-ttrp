# Second Word : Lah (Magicka)
---
- **Uttering time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30-foot cone
- **Duration:** 2 minutes

The creature must make a Constitution saving throw or suffer the effect of the first word, as well as lose the lowest level spell slot it has prepared.
