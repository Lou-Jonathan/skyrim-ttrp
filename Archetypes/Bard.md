# **Bard**
---
Bards are charismatic performers who use their charm, wit, and magic to navigate the world. Whether entertaining crowds in bustling taverns or weaving illusions to outsmart their enemies, Bards are masters of persuasion and deception. Their quick thinking and agility make them adept at escaping danger, while their silver tongues can turn foes into allies.
